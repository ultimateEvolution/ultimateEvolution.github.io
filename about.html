About UE

UE is an ultimate frisbee club in Adelaide. We compete in Adelaide city social leagues, women's divisions, men's divisions, and the mixed divsisions. Sometimes we organize and play pickup ultimate frisbee around Adelaide. If you are interested in joining us for any of these adventures, please send an email to contact@ultimateevolution.com
